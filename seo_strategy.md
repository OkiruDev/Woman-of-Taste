# SEO Strategy

- Public marketing pages
- Journal / blog pages
- Event hub, event detail, and location landing pages
- Experience and restaurant listing/detail pages
- Contact and partnerships pages

## Out of scope
- Authenticated and administrative routes under `/admin/**`
